How It Works

From idea to global scaleup — one seamless journey

No more juggling multiple portals. Our AI identifies where you fit, checks eligibility instantly, and pre-fills your application.

💬
Step 1
Talk to AI Copilot

Describe your startup in natural language. The AI assesses your stage, sector, and needs in under 2 minutes.
